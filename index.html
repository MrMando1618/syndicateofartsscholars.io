WHO WE ARE

SAS is a not-for-profit, student-led club, dedicated to creating a vibrant community at Monash within the Arts and beyond! From unforgettable social events to supporting students in developing industry connections, SAS is designed to help you get the most out of your university experience.

1.Establishing Student Initiative

Our vision extends beyond the present. SAS is committed to creating sustainable initiatives that future committees can carry forward, ensuring a lasting impact on the Arts community for years to come.

2. Build a Welcoming and Rewarding Community

SAS is dedicated to providing spaces where everyone feels welcomed, valued, and celebrated. Our events reflect the rich diversity of the Monash student community, and strive to ensure all students feel a genuine sense of belonging at Uni.

3.Facilitating Student-designed Academic Support

SAS wants to say goodbye to boring staff-led lectures and hello to engaging, student-driven academic events! We endeavour to bring you student testimonials, student-led panels, and networking opportunities that feel relaxed and fun.
